Draft System

Weekly drafts on Saturday afternoons

Pick order: reverse order from previous week's results, with the first pick dropping back to the last pick after each draft round

Rounds: the first pick of each round gets to decide which position is being drafted that round

Rules: your player must be an active starter on his team, an injured player on your roster wil cost you 10 points!
